# WaveFrame Template Club

Template Club is the long-term pack ecosystem for artists, labels, DJs, and streamers.

## Starter packs
- Release Announcement Pack
- New Music Teaser Pack
- Label Drop Pack
- DJ Stream Overlay Pack
- Album Art Motion Pack
- Presave Campaign Pack
- Tour Flyer Motion Pack
- Minimal Meter Pack
- Visual Loop Pack

## Monthly drops concept
- monthly template/tool drops
- pack refreshes and seasonal themes
- creator workflow upgrades over time

## Future expansion
- community preset packs
- premium/pro packs
- curated inspiration highlights

## Beta constraints
- local-only beta state
- no cloud sync
- no account backend
